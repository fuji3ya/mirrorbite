Two promises

Encrypted in transit, analyzed once, deleted. Your photo is sent over HTTPS, processed in a single AI call, and removed from our server immediately. We never store images or use them to train models.

We never invent numbers we can't see. When the AI is uncertain, the app says "I held back this one." That's not a bug — that's the feature.

Directional, not medical. Mirrorbite is not medical advice and not a substitute for a registered dietitian. Just honest categorical feedback (good / caution / low) — never grams, never calories.
